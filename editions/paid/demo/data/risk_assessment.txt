RISK-001: Single-Source Dependency — Industrial Automation (Mitsuya Precision)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Category     : Supplier concentration
Probability  : 4 (High — geopolitical tensions in East Asia)
Impact       : 4 (Critical — 100% of IA revenue dependent on this supplier)
Risk Score   : 16 (CRITICAL)
Description  : Mitsuya Precision is the sole qualified supplier for all three
               Industrial Automation SKUs. A disruption (earthquake, export
               restriction, or financial distress) would halt production with
               no alternative available.
Current controls:
  - 8 weeks safety stock maintained (above standard policy)
  - Quarterly financial health reviews of Mitsuya
  - Engineering assessment of alternative supplier initiated (Siemens AG)
Mitigation plan:
  - Qualify Siemens AG as secondary supplier by Q3 2025 (Owner: Supplier Dev)
  - Target: reduce Mitsuya share to 70% by Q4 2025
  - Emergency air-freight capability confirmed with DHL (adds $180/unit cost)
Residual risk after mitigation: Score 9 (High → Medium)

RISK-002: Geopolitical — US-China Trade Tariffs on Electronics
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Category     : Regulatory / geopolitical
Probability  : 4 (High — trade policy uncertainty elevated)
Impact       : 4 (Critical — 80% of CE sourced from China)
Risk Score   : 16 (CRITICAL)
Description  : A new tariff round or export restriction on semiconductor components
               from China could increase CE component costs by 15–25% and disrupt
               supply for 8–16 weeks during requalification of alternatives.
Current controls:
  - Tariff scenario modelling completed (5%, 15%, 25% tariff impact models)
  - Buffer stock build planned if tariff signals emerge (additional 4 WoS)
  - FlexPower GmbH (Germany) identified as partial alternative for EU market
Mitigation plan:
  - Accelerate FlexPower qualification for North America market (Q2 2025)
  - Explore Vietnam-based assembler (3 suppliers identified, RFQ in progress)
  - Tariff insurance policy under review with Marsh (decision by Feb 28)
Residual risk after mitigation: Score 12 (High)
