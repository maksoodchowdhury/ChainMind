# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple principle: be respectful, constructive, and professional.

## Expected behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and accept constructive feedback.
- Focus on what is best for the community and project quality.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Trolling, personal attacks, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from the project spaces.
